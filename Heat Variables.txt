The Heat Variables are used in the efficiency equation:
efficiency = 742*(e^(-t/h) + tanh(t/h) - 1),
where 't' is the current temperature of the fusion reactor and 'h' is the fuel combo's Heat Variable.
To find the temperature at which the efficiency is maximised, solve the following equation:
sech^2(t/h) = e^(-t/h).
